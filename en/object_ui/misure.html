1.3 Measurement

Datatype: Container

CA element code: measurements_CN

Repeatability: Yes

Quicktip: Repeatable fields for analytical measurements.

Usage examples: {to be completed}

1.3.1 Measurement Description

Datatype: Text

CA element code: measures_desc_meas_ME

Quicktip: Describe the type of analytical measurement. For example: "vibrating length of the string", "diameter of the membrane", "diameter of the digital holes", "distance of the holes from the mouthpiece", "distance of the holes from the labium".

Usage examples: {to be completed}

1.3.1 Measurement

Datatype: Length

CA element code: length_measurements_ME

Quicktip: If the measurement provided is a length, enter the numeral followed by the unit of measurement (units commonly used and accepted in the field are expressed in mm, cm or m).
