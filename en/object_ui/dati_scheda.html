1.3 Approval date

Datatype: DateRange

CA element code: app_date_ME

Quicktip: Enter the date of approval of the card. 'daterange' field, enter single dates or, for uncertain dates, time intervals. Examples: 'August 30, 1746', '1900', 'June 7, 2007 at 15:31:48', 'June 2016'. For uncertain dates, use the following formats: 'about 1955', '1955 ~ 3y' (1955 plus or minus 3 years), 'about 1950 - 1956', 'June 10, 1955 ~ 10d' (June 10, 1955 plus or minus 10 days), '850 BCE', '40 CE', '6/10/????' or '06/10/?' (date with day and month indicated, but no year), '6/????' (date with month indicated, but no year). The following values can also be used, which will then be transformed by the system into a range of actual dates: 'autumn 2003' (season indication), '20 Q3' (third quarter of the twentieth century).
