1.1 Edition / draft / version

Datatype: Text

CA element code: edition_indication_ME

Quicktip: Use one or more edition indications (e.g., '2nd ed.', 'Editio prima'). For unpublished resources, provide one or more drafting indications for manuscripts, sound recording or video recording indications ('take') for audiovisual resources, version indications for electronic format resources, and indications of transfer or reproduction for materials for internal circulation and preparatory materials or for copies, published or unpublished, in any form. Examples: 'Draft', 'Braille version', 'Complete scene shot with fixed camera', 'Linux version', 'Version 0.1.2', 'Master tape', 'Preprint', 'Conservative copy', 'Second draft', 'Take no. 3'.
