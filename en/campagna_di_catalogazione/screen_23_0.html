1.4 Campaign date

Datatype: DateRange

CA element code: campaign_date_ME

Quicktip: Daterange field. Enter single dates. Examples: August 30, 1746, 1900, June 7, 2007, June 2016. CollectiveAccess also supports date-range and uncertain date expressions in date-range fields, but it is advisable to use bibliographic standards, which express uncertain ranges through the DATA 1-DATA 2 method (see documentation).
