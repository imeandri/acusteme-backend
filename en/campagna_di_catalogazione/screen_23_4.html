1.1 Bibliography and sources

Datatype: Container

CA element code: bibliography_sources_CN

Repeatability: Yes

Quicktip: "List of publications and documentary sources used or produced as part of the research campaign. May include monographs, scientific articles, technical reports, theses, archival sources, or published ethnographic materials. Use in conjunction with the fields below for a structured description."

Quicktip: "Full citation of the source in a consistent bibliographic style (e.g., author, title, publisher, year). It is the main user-readable field and may be accompanied by identification codes and persistent links in subsequent fields."

1.1.1 Type of code

Datatype: List

CA element code: code_type_ME

Quicktip: "Specifies the type of identification code associated with the source. Examples: ISBN (for monographs), VIAF (for authorities), DOI (for articles and digital publications), ISRC (for sound recordings), ARK, Handle, URN (for digital resources in archives or repositories). The choice of type determines the validity and interoperability of the ""Code"" field. "
