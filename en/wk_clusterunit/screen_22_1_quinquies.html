1.1.1 Reel

Datatype: Integer

CA element code: reel_number

Quicktip: Identification number of the reel (reel) of the film with which the music cue is associated. Commonly expressed in digits (e.g., 1, 2, 3B), it corresponds to the physical subdivision of the film by audio/video editing units.

1.1.2 M designation

Datatype: List

CA element code: designation_M_ME

Quicktip: Alphanumeric field indicating the designation of the music track according to the M-R-Cue convention (e.g., 1M1, 1M3A, 1M5). "M" (Music) followed by number or letter identifies a coherent musical block, often associated with a scene or narrative section.
